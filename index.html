Tap-Deck gives you a clean, professional profile link you can share anywhere — plus optional physical NFC products that come pre-loaded to your profile.

One-time fee. No recurring costs.
Early adopters price: $15 (will increase to $25 soon).
Physical products are separate purchases if you want them.
Get started: Choose a theme Shop NFC products

What you get

A profile at www.tap-deck.com/your-slug with your logo (or photo), links, contact card download, and a QR you can share or print.

How it works

  1. Choose your theme.
  2. Pay once (or use a one-time token).
  3. Fill out the intake form.
  4. You’ll receive an email with your live link and QR.

Perfect for

Small businesses, vendors, events, real estate, service pros — and soon a personal “social” version for everyday use.

FAQ
Can I change themes later?

Yes you can updated all information via contacting support, but coming soon you will be able to make these changes on the fly when ever you want!

Are there recurring fees?

No — Tap-Deck profiles are a one-time fee. The only additional cost is if you choose to purchase physical products (cards, bracelets, chips, signage) pre-loaded to your profile.

What if my slug is already taken?

You’ll be prompted to choose a different slug. Your slug must be unique because it becomes your public link.

Do you offer NFC products?

Yes — visit the Shop for NFC cards, bracelets, chips, and display signage. All items can come pre-loaded with your profile.
