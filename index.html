FAQ

Can I update my profile?
Yes you can update all information via the edit page. You will need your edit code which you can request if you cant remember it below on the home page!
Do I need an NFC device?
Nope — your link works anywhere. NFC devices just make it easier to share with a tap.
How long does it take?
Usually just a few minutes. You’ll get an email when your profile is live. If a New Profile is not live or an existing profile is not updated wait at least five minutes then reach out to support.

Need your edit code?

Enter the email address on your Tap-Deck profile and we’ll send your edit code.
